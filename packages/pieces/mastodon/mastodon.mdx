## How to obtain access token for Mastodon
1- Go to your Mastodon instance
2- Go to your profile
3- Go to "Preferences"
4- Go to "Development"
5- Click on "New Application"
6- Fill the Information
7- Click on "Create Application"
8- Copy access token from "Your access token"
