## Create Incoming Webhook URL for Sending Message

- Log in to your Mattermost account.
- Click on the settings top left, and select integrations.
- Click on the "Incoming Webhooks" option.
- Create Incoming webhook, copy the url and add it activepieces.


<Tip>
To make certain configurations in your Mattermost account, you need to override the username and icon URL. 
You can find more information about this process by visiting the following link: https://docs.mattermost.com/configure/configuration-settings.html#enable-integrations-to-override-usernames.
</Tip>
