---

These are main concepts of activepieces.
* **Collection**: consists of flows and configs togethers.
* **Configs**: consists of predefined information such as authentication information, or text.
* **Flow**: consists of one trigger and followed by series of actions.
* **Trigger**: describe the trigger for the flow, could be schedule, webhook or third party app trigger.
* **Action**: describe the action, could be code snippet, store, third party app action.
* **Instance**: describe the state of collection whether it's enabled (currently running) or disabled and which version,
 there could be at most one instance of each collection.
